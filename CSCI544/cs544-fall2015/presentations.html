Getting materials to us

Email Mark and Kallirroi any slides you wish to project during your presentation on or before Sunday, November 29th. Given the large number of student presentations, all groups will be using the computer provided by the instructors which will have a copy of these slides. PDF format is preferred. We have PowerPoint 2010 for Windows but there is no guarantee that your slides will look the same on our computer as they do on your computer.

Your presentation

You have five minutes for your presentation so we suggest that a single member of the group give the presentation. Your first slide should be a title slide with the following information:

  • A title summarizing what the project is about.
  • Your group number (see the copy of the proposal we sent you or check the schedule).
  • All the names of your group members with the person presenting underlined.

You can include as many slides as you want after the title (but remember you only have 5 minutes). Text and pictures are fine but we will not be able to support video or audio. Given the short presentation time, we recommend that you quickly state the goal of the project, your methodology and your evaluation.

Report on presentations of other groups

You will need to take notes during the presentations. As part of your individual final report, you will need to summarize the presentations of ten other groups. For each group you will need to include answers to the following questions in your report:

  • What is the group's number?
  • What was the goal of the group's project?
  • How did the group attempt to accomplish the goal?
  • How did the group evaluate their work and what was the result?
